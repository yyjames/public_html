Point System

House competition The first 12 of each team have to count for points. The House with the least points will be the champion. In case of a tie between two teams, it shall be resolved in favour of the team whose last scoring member among the first 12 competitors finished nearer the first place. Team with less than 12 runners will be disqualified.
Class competition The first 3 boys and first 3 girls of a class have to count for points. The class with the least points will be the champion. In case of a tie between two teams, it shall be resolved in favour of the team whose last scoring member among the first competitors finished nearer the first place. Team with less than 6 runners (3 boys and 3 girls) will be disqualified.

Awards


House competition
  • Certificates - first ten places in each group
  • Medals - First, Second and Third places in each group
  • Overall Champion award - House with the highest total scores
  • The Most Participation Award - House with the highest percentage of competitors
Class competition
  • Prizes - First, Second and Third places in each group
  • The Most Participation Award - class in the two Senior groups with the highest percentage of participation
